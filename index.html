About Brad Traversy

Brad is the creator of Traversy Media and the instructor in all tutorials and courses.

Brad was a freelancer, worked for a company and a business owner, but He discovered his real passion is teaching web development & programming in a simple and understandable way. He now run the Traversy Media YouTube channel and sell online courses

Brad is very family oriented. He has a 12 year old daughter named Brianna and a 10 year old son named Nicky. His family is the most important thing in his life, after that is teaching and helping people learn new skills and better their lives
